```
CropDoc — Plant Disease AI for Every Farmer, Everywhere

Every year, plant diseases destroy 40% of global crops ($220B losses). 500 million smallholder farmers lack access to agricultural experts — and most AI tools work only in English, with an internet connection.

CropDoc changes that.

Built on Google's Gemma 4 E4B-IT (4B parameter multimodal model), CropDoc diagnoses 72 crop diseases fully offline, in 10 languages, in under 30 seconds.
```
